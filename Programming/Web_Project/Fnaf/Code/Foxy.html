Foxy

Foxy, the pirate fox animatronic, is known for its aggressive behavior and unique design. Foxy is one of the attractions at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, and its origin story involves its role as an entertainer for children.

Over time, Foxy's appearance becomes more unsettling, and players encounter this character in various FNAF games, adding to the overall suspense and horror of the series.

The character's backstory and involvement in the FNAF lore make Foxy a significant and haunting figure in the series, contributing to the overall mystery and fear experienced by players.

Foxy has several variants, each with its own menacing presence:

Funtime Foxy: A more colorful and playful variant, Funtime Foxy is part of the Sister Location game and brings a new level of terror with its entertaining appearance.

Mangle: Originally Foxy the Pirate, Mangle is a dismantled and broken version, adding to the horror with its disfigured appearance and erratic movements.

Phantom Foxy: A spectral and ghostly version of Foxy, Phantom Foxy contributes to the supernatural elements of the FNAF lore, haunting players in various games.
