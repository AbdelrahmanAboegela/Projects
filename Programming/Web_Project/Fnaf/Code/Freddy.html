Freddy

Freddy Fazbear, the main mascot of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, is a brown animatronic bear. Freddy serves as the leader of the animatronic band and is a central character in the FNAF series.

The origin story of Freddy is closely tied to the dark history of the pizzeria, and encounters with Freddy in various games lead to intense and suspenseful gameplay moments.

Freddy has several variants, each adding a unique element to the horror of the FNAF series:

Toy Freddy: A more modern and playful version of Freddy, Toy Freddy is part of the newer generation of animatronics and brings a different level of fear with its updated appearance.

Withered Freddy: Withered Freddy, worn and tattered, reflects the passage of time and neglect, adding to the haunted history of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.

Phantom Freddy: A spectral version haunting the dreams of security guards, Phantom Freddy introduces supernatural elements to the FNAF lore.

Nightmare Freddy: As part of the Nightmare animatronics, Nightmare Freddy is a more sinister and terrifying version, instilling fear in the dreams of players.

Freddles: Tiny versions of Freddy that accompany Nightmare Freddy, Freddles contribute to the overall nightmare-inducing experience.

Golden Freddy: A mysterious and ghostly variant, Golden Freddy is known for its unexpected appearances and unsettling presence in various FNAF games.

Shadow Freddy: A shadowy and enigmatic variant, Shadow Freddy adds to the eerie atmosphere of the FNAF lore, appearing in mysterious and haunting circumstances.
