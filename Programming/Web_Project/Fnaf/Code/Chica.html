Chica

Chica is another animatronic character in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. Originally designed as a chicken entertainer, Chica is part of the ensemble of animatronics at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Chica has a distinctive appearance with its yellow feathers and a cupcake in hand.

Chica has undergone various iterations throughout the FNAF series, each bringing its own set of chilling experiences:

Original Chica: The iconic yellow animatronic chicken with a cupcake, contributing to the whimsical yet eerie atmosphere of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.

Toy Chica: A more modern and sleek version, Toy Chica features a cleaner design and serves as part of the newer generation of animatronics entertaining the pizza place's visitors.

Withered Chica: Withered Chica, worn and tattered, adds to the haunted history of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Its appearance reflects the passage of time and neglect.

Phantom Chica: A spectral version haunting the dreams of security guards, Phantom Chica introduces supernatural elements to the FNAF lore.

Rockstar Chica (Bonus Variant): While not an original Chica variant, Rockstar Chica brings a new level of entertainment with its rockstar theme, showcasing the diversity of animatronics in the series.
