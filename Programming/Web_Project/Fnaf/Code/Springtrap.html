Springtrap

Springtrap is one of the main antagonists in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. Originally known as Spring Bonnie, this animatronic served as an entertainer at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. However, after the tragic events of the past, it became possessed by the vengeful spirit of William Afton.

Springtrap is prominently featured in Five Nights at Freddy's 3. It is a heavily damaged, greenish-yellow bunny animatronic with visible human organs and a rotten appearance. Springtrap is known for its eerie movements and relentless pursuit of the player throughout the game.

The character's backstory and involvement in the FNAF lore make Springtrap a central and haunting figure in the series. Players encounter Springtrap in various FNAF games, each time providing a new layer to the character's dark narrative.

Springtrap has several variants, each contributing to the horror of the FNAF series:

Spring Bonnie: The original form of Springtrap, known for its entertaining past at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza before being marred by tragedy.

Phantom Springtrap: A spectral version, Phantom Springtrap haunts the player's experience with its ghostly presence, adding a supernatural element to the FNAF lore.

Scraptrap (Bonus Variant): Also known as Salvaged Springtrap or Springtrap 2.0, this variant appears in the later FNAF games, displaying a more withered and deteriorated state, further intensifying the horror.
