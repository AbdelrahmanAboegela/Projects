Bonnie

Bonnie is a blue animatronic rabbit that is part of the animatronic band at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. With its guitar in hand, Bonnie adds to the musical and entertaining aspect of the pizzeria.

Bonnie has evolved through various iterations in the Five Nights at Freddy's series, each with its own distinct characteristics:

Original Bonnie: The vibrant blue animatronic with a guitar, a classic member of the band, contributing to the nostalgic and eerie atmosphere of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.

Toy Bonnie: A sleek and modern version with large, expressive eyes and a redesigned guitar, entertaining a new generation of visitors.

Withered Bonnie: Bearing the scars of time and neglect, Withered Bonnie's worn appearance and missing faceplates add to the unsettling nature of this variant.

Shadow Bonnie: Also known as RWQFSFASXC, this mysterious and shadowy version symbolizes the darker and more supernatural aspects of the FNAF lore, providing players with cryptic encounters.

Springtrap (Bonus Variant): While not a Bonnie variant per se, Springtrap, originally Spring Bonnie, becomes a withered and grotesque animatronic possessed by the vengeful spirit of William Afton, playing a pivotal role in the haunting narrative of the series.
